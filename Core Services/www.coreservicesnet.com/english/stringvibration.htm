The String Vibration

   CORE SERVICES DE VENEZUELA, C.A., offers the service of Drill String Vibration Detection on Line as a power key for the RPM control and the PSM in order to avoid damage at the bottom assembly and in extreme cases, bankruptcy of itself.

Objectives of the Service:

  • To detect the possible vibration from the bottom assembly through a specialized software.

  • To optimized the RPM and-or the PSM in order to avoid damages at the bottom assembly.

  • Generate reports, graphs and numerical with the necessary information to make the decisions.
