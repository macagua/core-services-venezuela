Mud logging

   CORE SERVICES DE VENEZUELA, C.A., in the search of an operational pursuit and integral geological, complete and effective offers the service of mud logging, which combines the taking and analysis of channel samples with technology of vanguard in the instrumentation areas, systems and registry of formations evaluation.

Objectives of the service:

  • To monitoring and to register the drilling parameters in real time for the identification of attacks, gains or losses of circulation, tank levels, obstacle, control of trips, presence of high gas units.

  • It provides in real time the graphical information of the drilling parameters on the basis of time or depth.

  • It registers in real time the pore pressure and fracture gradient in perforated interval.

  • To detect and to characterize the chromatography of the formation gases that are gotten up to the perforation fluid, in order to be able to identify the prospective zones in the well.

  • To provide information with capacity to interact with atmospheres and platforms of software of contracting companies of our services with the purpose of facilitating the decision making.

   In order to fulfill such objectives, CORE SERVICES DE VENEZUELA, C.A., has some Mud Logging Units (cabins) designed and constructed to lodge operational pursuit and command teams, constitute excellent “Field Lab” for the collection of samples, geologic analysis and description of samples.

   In such sense the Units of Mud Logging are elaborated with materials of high quality with anticorrosive processing, have capacity for 5 people and exits of emergency, equipment of security and first aid.
