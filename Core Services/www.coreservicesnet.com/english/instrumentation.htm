Instrumentation

   The System of Instrumentation is conformed by a series of sensors and a system of data acquisition (data acquisition or PLC).

   The sensors located in the drilling rig emit analogical and digital signals, hat they are captured through a system of data acquisition in the cabin of Mud Logging, to process them through a software and via network, to show them to the company man, representative of the operating company. All this information can be accessed via Internet.

   Next a list of our sensors is provided, nevertheless, if the client has some specific requirement in the instrumentation area our platform is in the capacity to implement it.
