Integral Protection

   CORE SERVICES DE VENEZUELA, C.A. has a policy to consider of vital importance the prevention of the risks of accidents in all the activities of work and in such sense, this one is an inherent responsibility of all the personnel, for that reason it has like norm, to plan and to organize its activities of a safe way, in form so to take them until its culmination, without no type of events nonwished.

    The primary target is the one to preserve physical integrity and the life of the workers, on a par to guarantee the preservation of the goods that form the patrimony of the company and the environment in which this one develops.

   In conclusion, the observance and fulfillment of our policy of security, and the processing of plans of contingencies will allow the personnel to detect and to control any risk on time, which will affect the accident prevention or possible damage to third and also, will allow Core Services of Venezuela, C.A., to continue staying like efficient and productive company within the national oil industry.
