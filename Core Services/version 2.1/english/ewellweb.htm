The well on line is... e-WellWeb

Monitoreo and Trasmisión of data through Internet

   CORE SERVICES DE VENEZUELA, C.A., has the capacity to transmit automatically the data handled by our software SPP-S21 and in real time to our Web server so that it can be accessed from a computer located anywhere of the world and visualized through their navigator of Internet (Web browser).

   The well in line is a service created by  CORE SERVICES DE VENEZUELA, C.A. for its comfort through Software e-WellWeb, which allows to show and to process the information from a well in real time till its PC, providing a clear and objective vision of the operations conducted in rig.

   e-WellWeb provides a window to the well using a Web dynamics page with graphical interface that integrate valuable information of the geologic operations of drilling and according to the pre-established configuration previously by you according to your requirements:

With e-WellWeb you can visualize:

  • Data sensed and calculated in real time

  • Graphics per Time

  • Graphics by Feet

  • Graficas de Presiones

  • Graphics of Distribution of Time by each one of the activities conducted during the Drilling of an Oil Well.

  • Numerical per Time

  • Numéricos por Pie

  • Formable Reports

  • Masterlog in line

e-WellWeb additionaly offers to you:

  • Auxiliary Programs:

    • Hydraulics

    • Swab and Surge

    • Well Control

    • Conversion of Units
