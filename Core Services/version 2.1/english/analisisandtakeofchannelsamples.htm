Analisis and Take of Channel's Samples

   CORE SERVICES DE VENEZUELA, C.A., With the objective to make a geologic pursuit of the well perforation it offers the service of Taking of Samples of Channel.

   The main purpose of this services is the collection, analysis and description from the formation samples in order to determine the crossed geological zone. In this way, we get enough information to generate litologs, and well correlations fundamental even to make decisions at the time of drill oils wells.
