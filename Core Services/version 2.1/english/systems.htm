Systems

   In the Area of Systems, CORE SERVICES DE VENEZUELA, C.A., has a software called SPP-S21 “System of Drilling Oil Century XXI” specially designed TO MAKE the FOLLOW-UP OF the PARAMETERS OF DRILLING OIL IN REAL TIME, that captures and processes the signals (analogical and digital) of the different sensors located in rig from continuous way (ON LINE).

    Parallelly they show and they are graphic providing therefore the vital information in the processes of decision making during the phases of drilling with which, the risks at the time of drilling an oil well are diminished.

General Characteristics:

Concept

  • It is a Software that works like a package of integrated services 100% formable one (by the user) as far as drilling parameters, graphs, reports, alarms of time for each one of the activities carried out during the drilling of an oil well.

  • It has an open architecture that provides flexibility in its design and configuration.

System of Data acquisition

  • Real Time

  • It has the capacity to work with P.L.C. (PLC) or any other system of data acquisition analogical or digital, since it can receive a limitless number of signals, because it is designed to add in versatile and fast form new sensors.

  • With capacity to interact with any mark and type of chromatograph FID or TDC.

Design

  • Capacity of Interfax API with friendly design and excellent presentation adapted to the visual requirements of the operations.

  • Designed in English and Spanish, with capacity to adapt it to any other language.

Parameters and modulates offered:

  • Sensed Parameter.

  • Parameter Calculated.

  • It has a generator of formulates to create, to modify calculations on the basis of the variables handled by the SPP - S21 “System of Oil Drilling Century XXI”.

  • Sensed and calculated data in real time.

  • Graphics per time.

  • Graphics by Feet.

  • Distribution graphic of the time for each one of the activities carried out. During the perforation of an oil well.

  • Numerical per Time.

  • Numerical by Feet.

  • Conformable Reports.

  • Masterlog on line

Alarms

  • Sonorous and visual

Graficas

  • Animated graphic completely conformable as far as tendencies, colors and scales.

  • Capacity to add commentaries on line in the graphs being allowed to emphasize excellent eventualities in a certain time.

Storage and Transportability of the data

  • Storage of data on the basis of drilled feet and time.

  • Consultation of numerical and graphical historical data

  • Registry of continuous Litologic with its respective description.

  • Temporary and definitive files.

  • Capacity to import or to export data in formats, DBF, XLS, TXT.

Access to Internet

  • For its comfort the data handled by the Spp-s21 is automatically transferred and in real time to a Web server of CORE SERVICES DE VENEZUELA, C.A.,soon to be accessed from a computer anywhere of the world through a called software e-WellWeb.

  • Protocol WITS (Well Site Information Transfer Specification).

  • email in e-WellWeb.

  • Protocol TCP/IP, FTP.

  • Compatible with navigators of Internet Explorer and Netscape.

  • Transportable files in ASCII, TEXT, EXCEL, DBase.

  • As far as Security use the last technology of encriptation to protect the data and the confidentiality of the information of our clients.

    • Hydraulic.

    • Well Control
